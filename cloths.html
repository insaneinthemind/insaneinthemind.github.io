The shirt is made with Printed Circuit Fabric, which was created by Xenoma’s materials and manufacturing processes. The material has stretchable wires and sensors integrated onto traditional textiles. The shirt features 14 sensors but are all hidden within the fabric, allowing users to wear the shirt like an everyday, regular shirt.
